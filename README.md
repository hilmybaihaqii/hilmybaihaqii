### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a Telecommunications Engineering student at Telkom University and a Research Assistant at the Cyber Physical System (CPS) Laboratory. I specialize in building robust web platforms, designing intuitive user experiences, and exploring data-driven infrastructure through Machine Learning and IoT.

- 🔭 Currently developing **Citra Banjir**, an early flood warning system in collaboration with BBWS Citarum.
- ⚙️ Preparing for an upcoming role in Virtualization and SDN Operation at **PT Telkom Infrastruktur Indonesia**.
- 🧠 Researching a Machine Learning-based prediction model for drug cardiotoxicity (Torsade de Pointes) utilizing Stacking Ensemble architectures and ADASYN.
- 🤝 Designed the UI/UX and led frontend development for **MIRA**, an AI-powered educational ecosystem.
- 🌐 Developed **AQUA** (IoT flood detection & soil monitoring) and **D'MOUV** smart home applications.
- ⚡ In my free time, I maintain an active lifestyle and regularly run 10km routes for stress management and endurance.
